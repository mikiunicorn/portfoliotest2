About

I'm Miki, a product designer with a decade in professional kitchens and a growing obsession with digital craft.

The same precision that goes into a perfectly layered cake goes into every interface I design: attention to detail, a deep understanding of the person on the other side, and zero tolerance for anything that doesn't serve a purpose.

I design across product, UX, and visual, from complex B2B platforms to consumer-facing experiences. I also create presentations, videos, and whatever it takes to bring ideas to life and communicate them clearly - from concept to final delivery.
